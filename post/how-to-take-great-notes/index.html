How to Take Great Notes

2020-03-18 1 min read # english # 科普

First, write down your conclusions instead of a barrage of facts.
Second, remember your notes visually.
And third, go over notes by teaching someone.

Step1

Don't write facts. Write conclusions!

    Answer-
  2. Question-

Step2

Use colored pens.

red - questions
blue - definitions
green - conclusions

Step3

Review your notes, don't relearn them.
